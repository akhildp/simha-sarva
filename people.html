    Ankit Sarwa

    Founding Partner

    Ankit S. is a founding partner at Simha & Sarwa Associates, bringing a rare depth of insight to the firm’s litigation and advisory practices. His career is defined by a distinguished transition from the judiciary to private practice, providing clients with a strategic advantage rooted in first-hand adjudicatory experience.

    From 2021 to 2025, Ankit served as a Judge in the Telangana Judiciary, presiding over matters in Hyderabad and Rangareddy. This tenure allowed him to master the nuances of legal interpretation and courtroom strategy from the perspective of the bench. Prior to his judicial service, he honed his skills in high-stakes litigation at Dua Associates, Bangalore.

    An academic standout, Ankit holds a B.A. LL.B (Hons.) from the Rajiv Gandhi National University of Law, Patiala. He further specialized at the Indian Law Institute, New Delhi, where he graduated as the Batch Topper and Gold Medalist (LL.M). Today, he leverages this rigorous academic foundation and judicial background to lead the firm’s focus on dispute resolution, real estate, corporate law, and complex civil and criminal matters.

    He regularly appears before High Courts in India and has advised domestic and international clients across multiple sectors.

    Keerthi Simha

    Founding Partner

    Keerthi Simha is a Founding Partner at Simha & Sarwa Associates, where he integrates a decade of substantive experience within the justice delivery system into strategic legal practice. His unique perspective is shaped by his tenure as a Civil Judge and Magistrate, a role that provided him with an "inside-out" understanding of judicial decision-making, evidentiary appreciation, and procedural rigor.

    A scholar-practitioner, Keerthi is an alumnus of the prestigious NALSAR University of Law, where he earned his B.A., LL.B. (Hons) and is currently pursuing his Ph.D. This enduring engagement with legal scholarship allows him to bridge the gap between complex legal doctrine and courtroom realism.

    At Simha & Sarwa Associates, Keerthi leads a practice distinguished by analytical depth and intellectual integrity. His work spans civil disputes, criminal litigation, real estate law, and arbitration, offering clients a composed, resolution-centric mindset. He is dedicated to building a practice rooted in long-term legal guardianship and unwavering ethical standards.
